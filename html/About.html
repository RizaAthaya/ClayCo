Grow Your Skill with Clay Course

What is Clay Course?

Clay course is an online class to develop skills using clay. On this website there will be a registration form to take part in the CL.CO class, purchase of tools and materials, as well as a short video about an overview of CL.CO classes and promos.

VISI

Provide opportunities for participants to learn and develop skills in making handicrafts from clay. With the clay course, participants can explore their creativity and learn different techniques in making handicrafts from clay.

Misi

Providing a fun and in-depth learning experience for participants so they can develop skills in making handicrafts from clay. In carrying out its mission, the clay course offers several activities, such as an introduction to tools and materials, basic carving, modeling, and finishing techniques.

TUJUAN

Encourage participants to develop creativity and interest in arts and crafts. In this case, participants will be given the freedom to experiment with various shapes and techniques in making handicrafts from clay, so that they can create unique and personal works.
