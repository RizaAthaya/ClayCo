The Tools

Clay

Type of earthy material that is soft and plastic when wet and hard when heated or fired

Rolling pin

A cylindrical utensil used to shape and flatten dough

Sculpting tools

Instruments to shape the clay. Such as pens, styluses, blades, knives, and sponges

Cutters

A tool used in pottery and ceramics to cut and shape clay into various forms

Silicone mat

An ideal surface when working with polymer clay because of its non-skid properties

Pottery wheel

A machine used in the shaping (known as throwing) of clay into round ceramic ware
